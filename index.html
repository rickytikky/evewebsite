Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

At Eve Intelligence ("we," "our,"), we prioritize your privacy. This policy details how we handle data from integrations like Kivra, Banking APIs, and HealthKit.

1. Data We Collect

  • Financial: Transaction history via open banking APIs (e.g., Klarna) for budgeting.
  • Health: Activity and sleep data via Apple HealthKit.
  • Admin: Invoice details via Kivra.
  • Social: Limited profile data from connected social apps.

2. How We Use Data

We use data solely to provide AI insights. We do not sell data to third parties or advertisers.

3. HealthKit Compliance

We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or data mining purposes other than improving health management.

4. Security

We use AES-256 encryption. Credentials are never stored on our servers.
